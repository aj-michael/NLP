Fillmore was the only Whig president that did not die in office or get expelled from the party, and Fillmore appointed the only Whig Supreme Court Justice. Fillmore was a lawyer from western New York state, and an early member of the Whig Party. As Zachary Taylor's vice president, he assumed the presidency after Taylor's death.
